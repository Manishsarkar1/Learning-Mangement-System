Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Designed from scratch for real learning — not just content delivery. Every feature earns its place.

📚
Courses & Assignments

Instructors publish structured courses with lessons, resources, and deadlines. Students track progress automatically — no chasing required.

✏️
Quizzes & Grading

Build multi-choice quizzes with instant grading. View per-student breakdowns, identify weak spots, and export results with one click.

💬
Announcements & Chat

Broadcast to your entire class or chat one-on-one. Threaded discussions, pinned updates, and real-time notifications keep everyone in sync.

📈
Progress Tracking

Live dashboards for students and instructors. Track completion rates, quiz scores, time-on-task, and engagement — at a glance or in detail.

How it works

Up and running
in minutes.

No lengthy onboarding. No implementation team. Just sign up and start.

01
Create your account

Sign up as an instructor or student. Invite your team in bulk or let them join via a course link. Role-based access is automatic.

02
Build or enroll

Instructors create courses and quizzes in minutes. Students browse the catalog and enroll with a single click — no approval queue.

03
Learn and improve

Track everything in real time. Quizzes auto-grade. Announcements notify instantly. Progress updates as students complete lessons.
